With multiple stops at major attractions, the Hop-on, Hop-off bus runs in a loop between Hollywood and Santa Monica every two hours. This is the best way to explore the city! This tour allows you to get on or off the bus at any of our stops. You can hop off between sights as often as you like to explore popular landmarks, dine at LA’s hotspots, try and get a glimpse of Hollywood celebrities, or shop at the many high fashion stores along the way. When you are ready, just hop back on the next bus and continue your tour.

We stop on Hollywood Boulevard, home of the legendary Walk of Fame and breathtaking Chinese Theatre. Grauman's Chinese Theatre is where Hollywood's most famous celebs create concrete hand and footprints when they're given a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. Wander the courtyard at Grauman's to see the hand and footprints of Tinseltown's most famous icons, like Marilyn Monroe and Groucho Marx, or walk down Hollywood Boulevard to see more stars.

Then we drive through Bel Air, one of L.A.’s most glamorous neighborhoods, and cruise down the Sunset Strip, where historic rock ‘n roll clubs – like the Whiskey A Go Go and Viper Room – stay open late. Next up is Beverly Hills, where you can shop at exclusive name brand boutiques on Rodeo Drive. Finally, we explore the glitzy neighborhood of Brentwood and the bustling Westwood Village – home to the UCLA Bruins. You’ll even get a chance to visit one of our beautiful beaches, glimpse the majestic Pacific Ocean and wander the Santa Monica Pier with its famous, gigantic Ferris Wheel.

Come join our Hop-On, Hop-Off Bus and discover L.A. in a brand new way! Don’t forget your camera!
