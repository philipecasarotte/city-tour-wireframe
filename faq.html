FAQ

How do I book a tour?

Booking a tour is very easy. On the menu on the left, click on "Tours". Then on the Tours page select the tour that interest you, and that will take you to the tour description page. On this page, you can select the date, time and number of adults and children attending the tour, then click the "Add to my Itinerary" button. At this point you can select to check out and pay for your tour, or you can select other tours to add to your order.

How do I book multiple tours?

When selecting several tours, there is no need to check out and pay several times. Once you complete the process listed above, simply go back to the tours page, and follow the steps to add another tour to the itinerary. You can add as many tours, and as many dates as you which. once you have made all of your selection, you can then check out, and complete one payment for all different tours you have selected.

Are Credit Card Transactions secure on this site?

LAsightseeing.net provides a secure shopping environment for its patrons. LAsightseeing.net utilizes SSL (Secure Sockets Layer), the most widely used encryption technology. SSL encrypts your credit card number by transforming the data into an unreadable format. Only an authorized processor can convert this information to complete the transaction. As well, through our partner Merchant Account, the site uses digital certificates, secure commerce servers and authentication to ensure that your personal information is secure online. LAsightseeing.net holds digital certificates from VeriSign, Inc., a trusted third-party Certification Authority (CA). Also known as Digital IDs, these digital certificates are similar to your driver's license or membership card in that they prove your identity. Digital IDs are useful in electronic commerce because they verify the identity of the parties involved.

Therefore, with all of this security, making a credit card purchase at LAsightseeing.net may actually be more secure than using your credit card at the local store or restaurant.
