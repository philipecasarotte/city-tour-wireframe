About Us

If you want to tour Hollywood in style, we've got the perfect adventure waiting for you. Since 1994, we have been providing visitors to Los Angeles with an exciting selection of guided tours that highlight the city's most famous sights, exclusive shopping districts and wealthiest neighborhoods. Some of the sights you can tour include the Hollywood sign, Rodeo Drive and Beverly Hills, the Walk of Fame, the beaches and Pacific Ocean, Bel Air, movie star homes and much more.

As a member of both the Los Angeles and Santa Monica Convention and Visitors Bureaus, you can count on us to provide a memorable and rewarding experience. Our knowledgeable bilingual tour guides always provide an entertaining experience, offering an interactive sightseeing tour of Hollywood that is as informative and enlightening as it is enjoyable. Guests ride in comfort and style; our fleet of vehicles includes air-conditioned motor coaches, open-air mini buses, full-sized vans, luxury sedans and limousines. We even offer our customers the chance to tour Hollywood in a helicopter!

The 16 standard tour packages we offer feature convenient departure times throughout the day, enabling our guests to tour Hollywood when it's convenient for them. Because we keep our tour groups small, you always enjoy a personalized experience, but if you want to arrange a custom tour for your group, we're happy to accommodate you. We're also pleased to offer guests foreign-language tour guides for international visitors. Simply give us a call to arrange service and we'll handle all the details.
