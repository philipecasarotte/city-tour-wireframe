Charter Service by GTS

Hopping on board a Hollywood tour bus is the perfect way to take in the sights and sounds of Los Angeles, playground of the rich and famous. We offer our valued guests a full range of touring options, and our friendly and knowledgeable guides provide you with an interactive sightseeing experience that is both informative and entertaining.

Find Out About Our Hollywood Tour Bus Packages!

Our fleet of late-model vehicles includes both air-conditioned coaches which offer maximum comfort as well as open-air mini-buses that let you soak up the glorious Southern California sunshine. We also use full-sized vans as part of our convenient charter services.

In addition to both scheduled and custom Hollywood tour bus services, we are pleased to offer general transportation services throughout the Los Angeles area. We offer door-to-door pick-up and drop-off service to groups of any size, at any time and for any event, including business meetings, conventions, and weddings.

You can also opt to take advantage of our luxurious limousine and private sedan services; our coverage area includes not only the Los Angeles area, but the entire southwestern United States.

We've been looking after the sightseeing and travel needs of visitors to Southern California since 1994, and we'll be happy to provide your family or group with the same high level of service that thousands of satisfied customers have already enjoyed.
